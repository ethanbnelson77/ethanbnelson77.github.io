Design Principles

Proximity

Los Angeles Times

https://www.latimes.com/

This website shows good use of proximity as it allows for a good amount of space between different sections. Things are grouped closely with things they are related too and very visibly separated from those they are not.

Alignment

DoorDash

https://www.doordash.com/

The DoorDash website is a good example of alignment in web design, as there are distinct columns that are easy to keep track of down the page. The content stays in a nice neat column that is easy to follow and is pleasing to the eye.

Contrast

Metal and Gas

https://metalandgas.com/

Contrast is showcased very nicly on this website. The general color of the website is a nice grayscale which allows the colors on the cars to really stand out and make the website as a whole very eye catching. Not only do the cars stand out, but the contrasting color choice makes for a very clean looking website.
